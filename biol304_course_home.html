03

Your Four Weekly Tracks

Every week pulls from the same four tracks. The Rhythm Card has the full version.

Lecture

Concept videos + quiz

About 90 minutes of videos in 4 to 6 short chunks. Weekly quiz, two attempts, best score kept. Due Sunday.

Discussion

Weekly async forum

Prompt opens Wednesday. First post due Friday so classmates have the weekend to read. Replies due Sunday.

Trainer

Your live note-taking

One printable trainer per week, built around the concept videos. Fill in each section as you watch. Due Sunday.

Lab

Anatomy + physiology

Two parts, one submission. Label anatomy figures and work through that week’s clinical tool. Due Sunday.

04

Your Clinical Reference Portfolio

Built one page at a time across the eight weeks. A pocket reference you actually keep.

What it is

Each week, you’ll add one page to a growing document: the system you studied that week, the clinical tool you learned, and how you’d explain it to a layperson. By Week 8 you have eight pages you can pull out on the job.

Part A

The anatomy

A labeled diagram of the week’s system, hand-drawn or annotated.

Part B

The clinical tool

What it measures, how to use it, and what the numbers mean.

Part C

Plain-language explanation

How you’d explain it to a patient, family member, or classmate.

Worth 20% of your final grade. Template and submission instructions will be posted in Module 0.
