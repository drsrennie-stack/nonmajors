Weekly Engagement Report

Each week, export the engagement report from your spaced recall deck and submit it here. It is how I see that you have been practicing, and it counts toward your engagement grade.

How to submit

Three steps, every week.

The same routine each week. Finish your reviews, pull the report, attach it here.

Finish this week's spaced recall

Open your deck and work through the cards due this week until the deck is current.

Export your engagement report

Your deck keeps a record of your daily activity. Download or screenshot that summary so you have it as a file.

Attach it and submit

Use the submit button on this assignment, below, to upload your report. One file is all you need.

Why it is daily

A little every day earns full credit.

Spaced recall works by spacing. A few minutes each day moves what you learn into long-term memory far better than one long session, and the report shows that pattern. Engaging with your deck every day is the surest way to full credit. A single cram the night before will show in the report, and it will not score the same.
