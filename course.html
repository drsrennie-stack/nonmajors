How the study sheets work

Every one-pager works the same way.

Learn the rhythm once and it carries you through all 44 sheets. Each sheet has three modes, plus a link out to spaced-recall practice.

Mode 01

Notes

Read the sheet in Notes mode for the full reference on the topic. This is the complete version you study from and return to.

Mode 02

Study

Study mode prints as a fill-in-the-blank worksheet. Complete it by hand as you watch the video or work through the reading.

Mode 03

Quiz me

Quiz me is on-screen typing practice for the vocabulary, so you can test your recall without printing anything.

Then

Spaced recall

Once you have watched the video, each sheet links out to its spaced-recall pre-work so the terms stay with you over time.

Meet your instructor

A decade of teaching the medical sciences, in your corner.

I'm Dr. Sharilyn Rennie. I've taught anatomy, physiology, and the medical sciences for over a decade across California community colleges, and before teaching I spent years in clinical medicine. That clinical background shapes how I teach: every structure connects to something real that happens in a body.

Anatomy and physiology is a language. My job is to help you become fluent in it, one module at a time, with a clear path and a steady weekly rhythm to follow. Come curious, come prepared, and reach out whenever you need help.

"Every structure has a story. We'll learn them together."
