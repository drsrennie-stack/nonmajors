Start labeling

How it works

  1. Load an image

    Pick a ready-made page from the starter library, or drop in your own unlabeled atlas image and answer key.

  2. Label by hand

    Draw your labels freehand with the pen tools. Switch to a dark background or grid, and zoom in for detail.

  3. Check yourself

    Toggle the answer key, or view it side by side, to compare your labels against the real thing.

  4. Build your book

    Save each pair as a page, group it by module, then shuffle through random pages to drill before lab exams.

Tip: Saved pages live in your browser on the device you used. Use Export book inside the tool to back them up or move them to another device.
