How this workbook works

Print, handwrite, scan, submit. This is the Anatomy Workbook for Week 1. Complete this first, then move to the Physiology Workbook for the dry lab.

Drawing tasks and synthesis questions are weighted heavily because they cannot be copied from AI. Numbered labels on figures check that you can identify. Drawings and synthesis questions check that you can build and integrate.

Word banks scaffold this first week. Starting Week 3 you will recall structures from memory without word banks.
