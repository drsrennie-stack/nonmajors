03

Build & Apply · Urinary Anatomy

The nephron

Drawings must be labeled to count.

Build

Build task. Draw a single nephron with its blood supply. Label all of the following: renal corpuscle (glomerulus and Bowman's capsule), proximal convoluted tubule, loop of Henle (descending and ascending limbs), distal convoluted tubule, collecting duct, afferent arteriole, efferent arteriole, peritubular capillaries. Use arrows to show the direction of filtrate flow.

Rubric: All five tubule sections labeled in correct anatomical order. Three vessels labeled. Filtrate flow direction shown with arrows.

Apply

Apply task. a. Renal artery stenosis reduces blood flow to one kidney. Predict the effect on filtration in that kidney and on systemic blood pressure. Trace the RAAS response from low renal perfusion to the BP outcome.

Apply

Apply task. b. A patient develops bilateral acute kidney injury after major hemorrhage. Predict three immediate consequences in the first 24 hours, and identify which would be the most life-threatening.
