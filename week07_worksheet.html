Calc & Build · TDEE and Label Lab

TDEE calculation and nutrition label audit

Lab anchor. Show your work. Round to nearest whole number for kcal values.

Mifflin-St Jeor formulas:
Male BMR = (10 × kg) + (6.25 × cm) - (5 × years) + 5
Female BMR = (10 × kg) + (6.25 × cm) - (5 × years) - 161
TDEE = BMR × activity factor (1.2 sedentary · 1.375 light · 1.55 moderate · 1.725 very active · 1.9 extra)
Calc

Calculation task. a. Calculate the TDEE of a 30-year-old female, 65 kg, 165 cm tall, sedentary office worker. Show each step (BMR formula substitution, BMR result, activity factor multiplication, TDEE result).
