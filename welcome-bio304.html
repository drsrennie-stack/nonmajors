One important thing

You've got one job, and this is it

Here's the good news: this class doesn't ask you to memorize a textbook or sweat through high-stakes exams. Your one job is to participate. Do that consistently and you are set up to succeed.

Participating means, every week:

  • Log in and engage with that week's material.
  • Submit your assigned documents and workbooks on time.
  • Complete the weekly quiz within its window.

I'll be straight with you, because that's only fair. I am required to drop any student who has not participated by the first day of class, and to drop students who don't keep participating through these opening weeks. I am also not flexible about late work. The deadlines are the deadlines. This isn't about being tough on you. It's the one rule that keeps a fast summer course fair for everyone in it.

So make it easy on yourself: log in on day one, do the work, and hit every deadline. That really is the whole game, and you can absolutely do it.
